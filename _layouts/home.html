Stefan's Story

I grew up in Berkeley, CA, then lived in New Haven and NYC. Today I'm living in Austin, Texas

  • beginnings

    A love of chemistry

    Like Oliver Sacks, my first love was chemistry. As a child I collected serpentine crystals in the California hills and hunted for fossils in Nevada.

  • education

    Yale University and University of Texas at Austin

    At Yale I studied everything I could: art history, literature, classics, philosophy... Several courses by Heinrich von Staden were influential and gave me an appreciation for the interplay between philosophy, technology, and medicine.

  • Teaching

    Undergraduate seminars in Science, Technology, and Society

    Topics included the origins of counting, writing, and computing, and human-computer interaction. Students were asked to reinvent and then use pivotal technologies: Renaissance-era perspective machines, and Turing machines made of toilet paper and post-its.

  • Today

    Product management and design

    I got my start during the first boom, and worked my way up working at startups and companies including The Economist, Google, Bloomberg.
